What Are We Learning Today?

Have you ever wanted to say what you, your friend, or your pet is doing at this very moment? Like "I am playing a game" or "My dog is sleeping"?

Today, you'll learn the secret English formula to do just that! It's called the Present Progressive, but let's just call it "Action Now" tense. It's super easy and fun!

Part 2: The Pronoun's Best Friend (The Verb "To Be")

Pronouns can't do the "Action Now" tense alone. They need a helper! This helper is the special verb "to be". It changes its form to match the pronoun.

Think of it like a key that only fits one lock!

Pronoun (The Lock üîí) Form of "To Be" (The Key üîë)
I am
You / We / They are
He / She / It is

Part 3: The Action Word! (Verbs + ING)

To show an action is happening NOW, we take a normal action word (a verb) and add -ing to the end. It's like giving the verb superpowers!

  • eat becomes eating
  • play becomes playing
  • read becomes reading
  • watch becomes watching
  • run becomes running (sometimes we double the last letter!)
  • dance becomes dancing (sometimes we drop the 'e'!)
